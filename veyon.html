The Problem

Current Lab Management Challenges

Faculty and IT staff face significant obstacles in managing large computer labs

No Visibility

Instructors cannot see what students are doing on their screens, making it difficult to ensure engagement and provide timely assistance.

Physical Movement

Faculty must physically walk to each computer to help students, which is time-consuming in large labs with 60+ students.

Limited Broadcasting

Difficult to demonstrate software and broadcast to all student computers simultaneously for effective teaching.

Software Management

Ensuring all computers have the same software versions and updates is a manual, time-consuming process.

Security Concerns

No way to lock student screens during lectures or prevent access to unauthorized websites and applications.

Power Management

No centralized way to power on, restart, or shut down multiple computers at once across the lab.

The Solution

What is Veyon?

Veyon is a powerful, free, and open-source computer lab management software that enables instructors to monitor and control all student computers from a single master computer.

100% Free & Open Source

No licensing fees or recurring costs ever

Cross-Platform

Works on Windows, Linux, and macOS

Trusted Worldwide

Used by thousands of educational institutions globally
