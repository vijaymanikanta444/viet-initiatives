Start From Today

2nd Year

Explore & Learn Basics

  • Pick one language (Python/C++)

  • Understand loops, logic, arrays

  • Solve 1-2 easy problems daily

  • Explore domains (Web vs Data)

3rd Year

Build & Profile

  • Build 2 good mini-projects

  • Participate in Hackathons

  • Learn Data Structures (DSA)

  • Create a professional LinkedIn

Final Year

Apply & Interview

  • Polish Resume & Portfolio

  • Practice Mock Interviews

  • Brush up Aptitude & Core CS

  • Apply aggressively

The Golden Rule: Start with 3060 Minutes / Day (Consistency > Intensity)
